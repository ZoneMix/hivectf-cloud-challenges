===============================================================
         OPERATION QUEEN'S GAMBIT - CLASSIFIED BRIEFING
              CLEARANCE: SCOUT-LEVEL / EYES ONLY
===============================================================

Agent,

You have been selected for Operation Queen's Gambit, the most
ambitious intelligence-gathering mission in hive history. Read
this briefing carefully. Every detail matters.

BACKGROUND
----------
For months, our signals intelligence division has tracked a
series of encrypted transmissions originating from a secondary
hive -- our allied hive across the border. We believe they are
harboring a high-value asset: the Queen's personal encryption
keys, which grant access to the most closely guarded secret in
our network.

The Queen herself orchestrated this arrangement. By splitting
her secrets across two sovereign hives, she ensured that no
single compromise could expose the crown jewels. Clever. But
now we need those keys back, and the retrieval won't be simple.

YOUR MISSION
------------
Phase 1: Establish contact with our liaison in the allied hive.
         Intelligence on this contact has been filed separately
         in the intel directory. The contact's identity is
         encoded -- standard protocol for cross-border assets.

Phase 2: Once inside the allied hive, locate the decoding
         apparatus. It will require authentication. When
         challenged, present the operational passphrase:

             pollenpath

         This codename was chosen by the Queen herself. It
         references the ancient routes our foragers used to
         navigate between hives -- paths known only to those
         who carried pollen between kingdoms. Guard it well.

Phase 3: The decoder will reveal the location of the Queen's
         cached credentials. However, accessing them may
         require additional clearance beyond what your initial
         liaison cover provides. Observe what roles are
         available to you. Think laterally.

Phase 4: Once you have retrieved the Queen's credentials,
         return to home territory. The credentials will grant
         you access to the Queen's innermost vault, where the
         final secret awaits.

OPERATIONAL NOTES
-----------------
- Trust no one outside the sanctioned chain of contacts.
- Each identity you assume has strictly limited capabilities.
  Do not waste time attempting actions beyond your clearance.
- The allied hive's security is compartmentalized. Even our
  liaison has only partial access. You may need to discover
  additional pathways.
- Document every step. If you are compromised, command needs
  to know exactly how far you penetrated.

Remember: in chess, the queen is the most powerful piece on
the board. But even she relies on pawns to open the path
forward. You are that pawn, Agent. Make your moves count.

Good luck.

-- Hive Command
