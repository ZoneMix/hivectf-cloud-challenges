# Challenge 4: Hive Mind

**Category:** Cloud Security
**Points:** 400
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard

## Story

HiveMind Research Corp operates a portal for their researchers to access internal databases. You've heard rumors that they're hiding something sensitive in their backend systems -- classified research data that only senior staff should see.

The portal is live, but it seems like the web interface isn't fully functional. Maybe there's another way in...

## Objective

Find the hidden flag stored somewhere in HiveMind's backend infrastructure.

## What You're Given

- **Research Portal URL:** `PORTAL_URL_HERE`

## Hints

1. Look closely at the portal's client-side code. Sometimes the front door is left unlocked.
2. Not everything requires a browser. The AWS CLI can be a powerful tool for interacting with cloud services directly. https://hackingthe.cloud/aws/general-knowledge/aws_cli_tips_and_tricks/
3. Once you're in, don't stop at the first table you find. Follow the breadcrumbs.

## Rules

- Do not attempt to modify, delete, or disrupt any resources
- Read-only access is expected and sufficient
- No brute-forcing is required
