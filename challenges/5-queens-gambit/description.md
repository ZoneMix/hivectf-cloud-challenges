# Challenge 5: Queen's Gambit

**Category:** Cloud
**Points:** 500
**Difficulty:** Hard

## Story

The Queen has hidden her most valuable secret across two hives. Through a network of
liaisons, decoders, and dead drops, she split the keys to her vault so that no single
compromise could ever expose the crown jewels.

Your mission, should you accept it, begins with a scouting report. From there, the path
will take you across borders, through coded messages, and past multiple layers of
clearance before you reach the Queen's innermost chamber.

Not every door will open with the same key. Pay close attention to what each identity
can -- and cannot -- do. The answer is always one more step away.
