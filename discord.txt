HiveCTF is a jeopardy-style competition hosted by MetaCTF and created by Dakota State university students. the 2026 competition will take place from 10am-6pm central on april 18.

to suggest a challenge idea:
create a post
describe the general idea and path to completion
tag the post with "needs review" and the relevant category tag(s)

then, a committee member will come by and review your challenge idea. if deemed acceptable, they will tag it with "needs QA", and they (and anyone else interested) will begin working with you to iron out details, challenge files, and areas of improvement. once sufficiently tested, a committee member will mark your challenge "complete", and it will be sent to MetaCTF and included in the final competition.
